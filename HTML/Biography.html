Faker's History

He started playing League of Legends in December 2011 when the Korean server opened. Faker initially played normal (casual) games but soon started to ranked play when the League of Legends matchmaking system could no longer find players of equivalent skill for him to play against. Under the name GoJeonPa he climbed the Korean ladder and became a strong mid-laner, known for his aggressive play and impressive mechanics. This is what started his carrer as a professional League of legends player.

Faker achieved the no. 1 ranking in solo queue and began attracting the attention of several Korean e-sports organisations. There were rumours of many offers from prospective teams but Faker decided to join SK Telecom T1 in February 2013 and played on their second team..

Faker is a player with true sportsmanship and respect.

FAKER Lee Sang-Hyeok

Faker Lee Sang-Hyoek was born in May 7th 1996 currently aged 20. He started off playing computer games at a young age starting off with Warcraft 3. He soon found League of legends in 2011 and became very good at it. He got recruited and decided to drop out of high school and join SKT to start his professional career. This was when his team won the world championships and was renowned as the best league of legends player in the world. This created many opportunities for faker Especially in terms in cash prizes and endorsements. For example he was offered W500Million equal to 441740 USD dollars just for streaming his gameplay.
